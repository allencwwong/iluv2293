City Hunt SF exciting to present city-based adventures that feel like a cross between The Amazing Race and National Treasure. By solving a series of fun and challenging clues, teams find their way from parks, to landmarks, to often-overlooked spots, racing to see who will be the first to solve the clues, find secret locations, and win the day.

How does it work?

We offer two types of hunt option scavenger and treasure hunt. Both requires players to solve clues, search for location and reteive prize item(s).

Scavenger Hunt

You'll have one hour to navigate through a swag-filled, motivational-poster-adorned startup office to "launch" before your faux investment money runs out. You'll have to complete clues in 5 categories before the time runs out. (Don't worry, you don't actually need to know any of these skills!)
