How does it work?

We offer two types of hunt option scavenger and treasure hunt. Both requires players to solve clues, search for location and reteive prize item(s).

Scavenger Hunt

You'll have max of three hours to navigate through around the city of San Francisco. You'll have to complete clues in 8 categories before the time runs out. You will have to solve for the final destination and team with the most clues completed and arrive earliest at the final destination will be the winners!

Treasure Hunt

You'll have max of 1 hour and 30 minute to navigate through around beautiful Golden Gate Park of San Francisco. You have to follow series of clues to locate 4 check points. The first team arrive at the final check point will be the winners!
